Jönköping

What To Do In Jönköping

If I only had one day to spend in Jönköping, I'd wander around the city, pop into a cafe for fika, browse in the shopping streets second hand shops and maybe in the evening find my way to the Folkets Bio to see a film. And without fail you will see the beautiful lakes if you just walk around.

Go Outside Or Go Out?

Places To Hike At

  • Strömsbergs Naturreservat
  • Rosenlund Rosarium
  • Utsikten Huskvarna
  • Dunkehallanleden
  • Hassafallsleden
  • Dumme mosse

Places To Eat At

  • In the summertime there is an excellent sushi place on a boat at the pier, but Icha Mochi also has a location that is open all year around.
  • Craving a good neapolitan pizza? Go to Ciro.
  • If you are not yet sick of the trendy burger maybe try Bada Bing Burger.
